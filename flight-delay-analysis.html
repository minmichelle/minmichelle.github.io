Why are Chinese flights in such a disordered environment? Surprisingly, the Hainan airline that I took from China to Seattle had been delayed for four hours. Top 10 Airport

First, I look for the top 10 airports in the US by adding up the departure flights and arrival flights together. Maybe next time we should consider leaving enough time if we transfer or travel to Atlanta. Another fact I found is the departure and arrival flights are not identical, however, there are only a slight differences that might happen for some unknown reasons.



Top 10 Airport Departure & Arrival Delay Ratio

Now it comes the most concern topic. How is the flight's performance in regarding delay? Will Atlanta be the most frequent airport for delay flights? I calculate the delay ratio by using the number of delay flights divide the total flight's number. Let me tell you the answer. It actually might not be what you have guessed. San Francisco actually keeps the highest delay ratio of almost 30% whereas Atlantic operated the best among all top 10 airports as you could see it only about 14% delay rate. So it is good to know we should be aware of traveling to LA and those big cities.



Airport Departure Delay Breakdown

If we further analysis the reasons for a delay, some unexpected reason came out in the chart shows that carrier delay and the late craft has been the two biggest reasons for the delay. Questions would be asked was what does carrier delay mean? To explain that, you need to know the U.S air carrier provides air transportation service and the data I gather in this article was provided by the Bureau of Transportation Statistics(BTS) for Airline Service Quality Performance System(ASQP) flights only. Here is the detailed explained of these five type of delay. (TYPE OF DELAY) http://aspmhelp.faa.gov/index.php/Types_of_Delay

It looks like the carrier delay includes some combined issues for internal maintenance and inspection of aircraft and logistics of crews, passengers, and baggage. Now, if you heard the radio calling for missing passengers to board or handling disabled passengers, yes, they all counts on the time delay. However, these occurrences were hard to avoid and quite understandable, especially in a big airport. Another big reason is the late aircraft. Why is it not due to weather? I would also raise the same questions as you are since that is the most common explanation we have been told. In fact, the similar pattern of these airports gives no support for explaining only weather leads to delay. Plus, the NAS delay becomes the third biggest reasons so we might have more concerns for how efficiency will the national airspace system operates.
